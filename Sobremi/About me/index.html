It all started during the pandemic in 2020, when I was searching for what I wanted to dedicate myself to. I tried courses on various topics and even pursued a degree to figure out what could be the perfect tool to shape my future while continuously advancing with each step I took. Then, in 2022, I discovered web design through an advertisement about a web design and development course. I started researching and learning about it, and that’s when I told myself, "I FOUND WHAT I LOVE TO DO." To this day, I keep improving and progressing constantly to create user-friendly, appealing, and adaptable projects for all devices.
